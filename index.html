GHAZALS

The ghazal represents a very important body of work in Urdu literature. It takes the legacy of Urdu poetry forward in this new age; its popularity truly knows no bounds with cinema greatly propelling its reach. Inspired by Urdu ghazals, many languages have tried to imbibe this form and have created beautiful ghazals in their own language. Ghazals in Hindi are examples of how it has flourished in another language. It has given rise to new themes and styles of composition . In this section, we have presented some ghazals which have become an integral part of Hindi poetry.
